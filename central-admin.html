Order Workflow

Every step from sale to retention — in order.

1
Customer buys

Buyer pays via Paystack. Confirmation email fires automatically. You get an alert email. The COA appears for the buyer. Add the order to your dashboard.

Automatic + add to Orders tab

2
Production (7–14 business days)

Send file to printer. Inspect quality. Sign and number the print. Package carefully in acid-free tissue + rigid backing.

Your action — physical

3
Ship & send tracking

Hand to DHL. Use Ship Order tab (or the button on the order card) to send the buyer their tracking number the same day.

Your action — Ship Order tab

4
Post-delivery follow-up

7–10 days after shipping, send a warm check-in. Ask how the print arrived. Invite them to share a photo. Builds loyalty and repeat buyers.

Your action — Follow-up tab

5
Retention

When new work drops, notify past buyers first. When a print sells out, collect waitlist names and notify via Waitlist tab when it restocks.

Your action — Waitlist tab
